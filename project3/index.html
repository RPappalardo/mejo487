Standout Years

2012

In 2012 there were 11 events costing more than $1 billion, including seven severe tornados, two hurricane/tropical storms, and a year-long drought including many wildfire. Sandy was the most expensive disaster of 2012 totaling $65 billion in damages.

2005

2005 was the most mostly year since 1980 due primarily to 4 large hurricanes including Katrina. Damages in 2005 were $160 billion (2005 dollars)/$190.4 billion (2013 CPI adjusted).
